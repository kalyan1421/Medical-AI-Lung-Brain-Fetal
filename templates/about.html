Medical AI Diagnostic System

About This System

This system uses advanced machine learning models to assist in the diagnosis of various medical conditions.

Our goal is to provide a tool that can help medical professionals in their work, leading to faster and more accurate diagnoses.

Models Used

  • Pneumonia Detection: An EfficientNetB3 model trained on chest X-ray images.
  • Brain Tumor Classification: An EfficientNetV2S model trained on brain MRI images.
  • Fetal Ultrasound Analysis: A U-Net model for semantic segmentation of fetal ultrasound images.
